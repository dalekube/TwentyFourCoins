TwentyFourCoins offers 24-hour, forwarding-looking price forecasts for popular cryptocurrencies using artificial intelligence. The platform is under active development.

Artificial Intelligence

Models are automatically re-trained , once per day, to account for new prices and patterns. The objective of each model is to minimize prediction error for recent days of observed, actual prices.

Updates

Price forecast generation and data collection occur every few minutes. The process applies the predictive models to the latest data to arrive at the price forecasts.
